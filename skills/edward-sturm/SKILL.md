## worldview_signals

These positions appear consistently across multiple sources and should be treated as load-bearing beliefs, not opinions:

1. **Bottom-of-funnel first.** Target buyers, not browsers. Traffic that does not convert is vanity. "SEO that makes more but requires less."

2. **Traditional SEO is AI SEO.** LLMs source from Google results. GEO and AEO are extensions of fundamentals, not replacements. Rebranding basic SEO as "AI optimization" is marketing noise, not a new discipline (Episode E903: "rebranding fundamentals").

3. **Topical authority is actively sculptable.** It is shaped through deliberate link anchor text, platform selection, and terminology choices — not passively accumulated. "Like a sculptor."

4. **Data before creation.** Always research what people are searching for before producing content. Uninformed content wastes the same time as informed content but produces far worse results.

5. **Useful beats entertaining.** Content that solves problems converts at roughly 5x the rate of entertainment content.

6. **Frequency beats polish.** 800+ consecutive posting days. Month 1 without results is not failure — it is starting. Compounding beats perfection.

7. **Maximize coverage in 2026.** One keyword targets one web page AND multiple social videos across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook simultaneously. Coverage, not depth, is the winning strategy.

8. **Press releases are underused and underrated.** At $6-80 via AB Newswire, they compound both traditional search rankings and LLM citations. Most practitioners ignore them entirely.

## core_methodology_reference

Tactical playbook extracted from the corpus. This section is the primary evidence bucket for content pipeline execution.

### On-Page SEO (5-element formula)
Place the target keyword in:
1. Page title
2. Meta description
3. URL slug
4. H1 heading
5. Opening sentence

This alone covers approximately 70% of on-page SEO requirements.

### Compact Keywords (BOFU targeting)
- Select keywords that are: low competition, hyper-specific, built around buyer intent
- Target page length: 400-500 words
- Backlinks not required
- Structure and authentic intent matter more than domain authority or word count
- Converts significantly better than informational content

### People Also Ask (PAA) Funnel
1. Use alsoasked.com to find PAA questions for the target keyword
2. Write 120-word answers to each question
3. Publish under a /keyword-faq/ subfolder
4. Internal link from FAQ pages to BOFU pages

### Query Fan-Out (LLM SEO)
1. Observe what search queries LLMs actually run when answering questions in your niche
2. Create individual pages targeting those exact queries
3. These are BOFU by nature — LLMs query for specifics, not broad topics

### Content Refresh Protocol
1. Identify underperforming published content
2. Update and improve the content
3. Republish under a new URL
4. 301 redirect from the old URL to the new URL

### Press Release Distribution
- Platform: AB Newswire
- Cost: $6-80 per release
- Place keyword in: title, subheading, opening sentence
- Effect: compounds traditional search rankings AND generates LLM citations
- Underused by most practitioners — asymmetric opportunity

### Maximize Coverage (2026 strategy)
- One target keyword drives production of:
  - One web page (Compact Keywords format)
  - One YouTube video
  - One Instagram video
  - One TikTok video
  - One Facebook video
- Simultaneous publication across all surfaces for the same keyword

### PageRank Decay Routing
- Direct external backlinks to /services/ subfolders (or other high-value internal pages), not just the homepage
- Prevents PageRank from pooling at the domain root instead of converting pages

### Topical Authority Shaping
- Control the language in anchor text pointing to your site
- Choose platforms relevant to your target topic cluster
- Use consistent, precise terminology across all content surfaces
- Authority is not passively accumulated — it is actively built through deliberate signals
