    The Bottleneck

    Why Autonomous Systems Fail in Production

    The "Agent" hype cycle has ignored the physical reality of production environments. Most autonomous implementations collapse under three primary pressures.

    CRITICAL
    01

    Latency & Token Burn

    Naive multi-agent architectures and unoptimized LLM chaining incinerate API budgets while choking user experience. Reasoning is cheap; millisecond-critical execution is expensive.

    ADVERSARIAL
    02

    The Physical Firewall

    High-value data and last-mile execution points are guarded by Cloudflare, reCAPTCHA Enterprise, and origin-binding. Thin-wrapper agents freeze the moment they hit the physical firewall of the modern web.

    FRAGILE
    03

    System Drift & Fragility

    Without deterministic circuit breakers and cryptographic state-persistence, autonomous pipelines lack the "memory" to survive mid-operation crashes, leading to silent failures and infinite loops.
