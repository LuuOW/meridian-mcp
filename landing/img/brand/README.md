# Third-party brand assets

This directory holds **trademarked brand marks owned by their respective companies**, included to identify *compatibility* (e.g. "works with Grok") on the Meridian site.

## What's here

| Folder | Owner | Source |
|---|---|---|
| `grok/`, `xai/` | xAI | xAI brand kit |

## Rules of use (apply to all sub-directories)

- **Don't recolor.** Use the `_Light.svg` variants on dark backgrounds, `_Dark.svg` on light backgrounds. The `Block_*` variants ship with their own background.
- **Don't squish.** Preserve aspect ratio; respect each brand's clear-space recommendations.
- **Don't imply endorsement.** These marks are used to indicate compatibility / interop, not partnership or sponsorship. Phrasing like "Add Meridian to Grok" or "Meridian as a Grok connector" is fine; "endorsed by xAI" is not.
- **Replace promptly on takedown.** If the rights holder asks us to remove or alter usage, do it the same day.

If you're vendoring these to another fork: confirm your usage falls within the rights holder's brand guidelines (search "<brand> brand guidelines"), or strip them entirely.
