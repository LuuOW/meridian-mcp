HelioCast — solar irradiance triangulator

PSP measures the Sun directly; JWST observes the Sun indirectly via reflected sunlight off solar-system bodies (Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, …). Cross-correlating PSP solar-event signatures with JWST reflectance excursions at known heliographic positions gives a per-body irradiance proxy that we forecast 24 h ahead.

Pipeline

Seven cloud workflows. Each rerunnable on demand.

  1. helio-mirror-pull — PSP FIELDS + SWEAP/SPAN-I, JWST solar-system observations, JPL Horizons ephemerides.
  2. helio-mirror-register — heliographic registration of every measurement.
  3. helio-mirror-detect — PSP PVI events + JWST per-FITS aggregates.
  4. helio-mirror-coincide — PSP event × JWST observation matching at advection lead time.
  5. helio-mirror-calibrate — Lambertian-disk inversion → inferred-irradiance proxy per body.
  6. helio-mirror-forecast — 24 h per-body persistence × r² forecast.
  7. (future) ML residual layer once we have multiple visits per body.
