Thanks for your purchase. Stripe will send your receipt by email.

What happens next

Your API key (for Pro / Team plans) or your download link (for one-time products) is provisioned manually and emailed to the address on your Stripe receipt within 24 hours. If you don't receive it, reply to the receipt or write to lucas.kempe@icloud.com with your Stripe session ID.

Manage your subscription

Stripe sends every receipt with a “Manage subscription” link. Use that link, or write to lucas.kempe@icloud.com to cancel.
